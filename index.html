ABOUT US

Founded in Dubai in 2016, MEDPRO is a leading integrated healthcare solutions provider operating across the Middle East and India. We deliver high-quality products and services, driven by innovation and a commitment to meeting unmet clinical and research needs. With a focus on responsibility, quality, and sustainability, we support innovators and connect them with the right end-users. MEDPRO continues to explore advanced technologies to offer effective solutions for our clients and partners.
